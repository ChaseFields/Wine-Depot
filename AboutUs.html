The Depot Difference

Our Story

At Wine Depot, we love wine. It's just who we are. From celebrating some of life's greatest moments to unwinding after a hard day's work, wine is there, and so are we. Our direct access to California's best vineyards and unique crafting process allow us to bring the highest quality wine for the best price straight to your door. By growing it, making it, perfecting it, and shipping it ourselves, we cut out the middle man and ensure your wine receives all the care you expect from fellow wine lovers.
